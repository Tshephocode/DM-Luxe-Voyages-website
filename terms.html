Our payment policies are designed to secure your luxury travel experiences:

Deposit Requirement

All services require a deposit before bookings are confirmed. The amount varies based on the total package cost and supplier requirements.

Final Payment

Full payment must be received at least 60 days prior to travel, unless otherwise specified for certain cruises or peak season travel.

Payment Methods

Payments can be made via bank transfer, credit card, or other agreed methods. All transactions are processed securely.

Important Notice

Failure to make payments by the due dates may result in cancellation of services and forfeiture of deposits.

The client must notify the Agency of cancellations in writing. The cancellation date will be based on when written notice is received.

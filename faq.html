Contact Our Experts

How far in advance should I book a luxury vacation?

For our most exclusive experiences and during peak seasons, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. This ensures availability at premier properties and allows us to secure special privileges. Last-minute arrangements can sometimes be accommodated, subject to availability.

Do you create custom itineraries?

Absolutely. Bespoke travel planning is our specialty. Our luxury travel consultants will work closely with you to design a completely personalized itinerary that reflects your preferences, interests, and pace. We handle every detail from private transfers to exclusive access experiences.

What distinguishes DM Luxe Voyages from other travel agencies?

We offer unparalleled access to exclusive experiences, premium accommodations, and personalized service. Our partnerships with luxury hotels and local experts worldwide allow us to provide unique benefits such as room upgrades, spa credits, and private tours not available to the general public.

Can you accommodate special requests or celebrations?

Yes, we specialize in creating memorable moments for special occasions. Whether it's a honeymoon, anniversary, milestone birthday, or romantic proposal, we can arrange private dinners, special amenities, and unique experiences to make your celebration extraordinary.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and in some cases, payment plans can be arranged for larger bookings. All transactions are securely processed, and we offer multiple currency options for international clients.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policies vary depending on the suppliers and properties involved. Generally, deposits are refundable up to 90 days before travel, with decreasing refunds as the departure date approaches. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment against unforeseen circumstances.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we work with premium insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage options including trip cancellation, medical emergencies, evacuation, and baggage protection. We'll guide you to the most appropriate policy for your specific itinerary and needs.

Are there any hidden fees?

We believe in transparent pricing. All costs will be clearly outlined in your proposal with a detailed breakdown. Our service fee is included in the package price, and we'll inform you of any optional extras before adding them to your itinerary.

What COVID-19 safety measures do you have in place?

We partner with properties and experiences that adhere to the highest health and safety standards. We stay updated on entry requirements for all destinations and will guide you through any testing or documentation needed. Many of our preferred partners have enhanced cleaning protocols and flexible booking policies.

What should I pack for a luxury vacation?

We provide detailed packing lists tailored to your specific destinations and activities. Generally, we recommend versatile luxury attire that can transition from day to evening, comfortable yet elegant footwear, and any specific gear needed for special activities. We'll also advise on dress codes for exclusive restaurants or venues.

Will I have support during my trip?

Absolutely. You'll receive 24/7 concierge support throughout your journey. We provide a dedicated emergency contact number and have local representatives in each destination should you need assistance. We also check in during your trip to ensure everything meets your expectations.

Do you handle visa and passport requirements?

We provide detailed information on visa requirements and processing for your destinations, and can recommend reputable visa service providers. However, passengers are ultimately responsible for ensuring they have the proper documentation for travel.
